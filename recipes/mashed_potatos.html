Mashed Potatos

Description

This recipe details how to make delicious mashed potatos that everyone will love.

You should aim to make double the desired amout, so you can have some leftovers for tomorrow.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Starchy Potatos
  • 200 ml whole Milk
  • 50 g Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Instant Stock
  • 1 Pinch Nutmeg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the potatos, slice them in .5 cm slices
  2. Put the potato slices in slightly salted water and boil them for 12-15 minutes until soft.
  3. When the potatos are soft, discard the water, add the milk, butter and Stock.
  4. Add a pinch of nutmeg and mash the potatos until there are no big chunks left.
  5. If the mashed potatos are too firm, add some more milk and mix until fluffy.
