Boiling Water Recipe

Description

This recipe shows you how to boil a pot of water.

Boiling water is useful in many situations, for example for cooking pasta, making tea or getting second degree burns.

Ingredients

  • Water, desired amount
  • Some more water, because some will boil off

Steps

  1. Put the water in a pot, pan, kettle or cauldron
  2. Put the pot, pan, kettle or cauldron on the stovetop or a campfire over high heat
  3. Wait till the water starts to boil
  4. Enjoy a nice refreshing cup of boiling water
  5. Special Tip: If you freeze some boiling water, it keeps for a long time. This might come in handy when you're in need of some boiling water.
